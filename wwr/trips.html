Main Trips

Experience the ultimate adrenaline rush on our White Water Rafting Adventure along the Wild Rapids River.

Nestled in the heart of a lush, untouched wilderness, this river is renowned for its breathtaking scenery and heart-pounding rapids.

Perfect for thrill-seekers and nature lovers alike, our guided rafting trip promises an unforgettable journey through turbulent waters and serene, picturesque landscapes.

Available Trips

At Dry Oar Rafting Co., we offer a variety of rafting trips tailored to suit different skill levels and interests. Whether you are looking for a heart-pounding adrenaline rush or a scenic family adventure, we have the perfect trip for you:

Trip Description
Raging Rapids Rendezvous Embark on a high-energy journey through the Raging Rapids River, known for its intense Class IV and V rapids. This trip promises non-stop thrills as you navigate through churning waters and steep drops. Perfect for adrenaline junkies looking for an unforgettable white-water adventure.
Cascading Canyon Adventure Explore the breathtaking beauty of Cascading Canyon while tackling a mix of Class II and III rapids. This trip is ideal for families and beginners, offering a balanced blend of excitement and scenic tranquility. Marvel at cascading waterfalls and towering canyon walls along the way.
Thunder Torrent Expedition Brace yourself for the Thunder Torrent, a river famed for its powerful and tumultuous waters. This advanced-level trip features challenging Class V rapids that will test your rafting skills to the max. Feel the thunderous roar of the water as you conquer each rapid with your team.
Wild Waters Escape Escape into the wild on this immersive rafting trip through pristine wilderness. Navigate through a mix of Class III and IV rapids, surrounded by untouched forests and abundant wildlife. This adventure combines the thrill of white-water rafting with the serenity of nature.
Torrent Trek Challenge Take on the Torrent Trek Challenge, a multi-day expedition designed for experienced rafters. Journey through a series of demanding Class IV and V rapids, with overnight camping stops at remote riverside locations. This trip is a true test of endurance and teamwork.
White Wave Wilderness Dive into the White Wave Wilderness, an epic rafting adventure through some of the most remote and wild landscapes. Encounter a mix of Class III and IV rapids, with ample opportunities to spot wildlife and enjoy the untouched beauty of the wilderness. Ideal for nature enthusiasts and thrill-seekers alike.
